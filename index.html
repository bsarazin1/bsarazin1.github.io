About

Born in Ft. Lewis, Washington. I grew up in Massachusetts. Joined the Airforce in 2009 originally as an airborne linguist but did not pass the final test at language school. Reclassed to a Geospatial Analyst. Reviewed imagery from Airforce air assets and made identification call outs for drone full motion video supporting ground forces in Afghanistan. I spent six months as a sensor planner, creating imagery collection flight plans for the U-2 spyplane. I also spent seven months in Afghanistan as a liason between my intelligence squadron in Virginia and the aircrew flying collection missions and coordinating with Army/Marine intelligence ground forces. I enjoy foraging, gardening and sailing. Currently living in VT.
